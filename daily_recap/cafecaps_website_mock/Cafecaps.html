Our Story

Coffee is Personal

We started Cafecaps with one simple belief: great coffee shouldn't be complicated or expensive. Just a perfect cup, every day, at home.

Cafe
Caps
2021Est. Bangkok

From a Small Kitchen to Bangkok's Best Kept Secret

Cafecaps began when our founder, a lifelong coffee lover, got frustrated with the high cost of branded capsules. After months of testing roasts, blends, and compatibility, we found the formula: premium coffee at a fair price, with capsules that fit your machine perfectly.

Today we're proud to supply thousands of Thai coffee drinkers every month, from our little shop on Sukhumvit 40 to doorsteps across the country.

"We don't just sell capsules. We help people fall in love with their morning ritual all over again."

— Cafecaps Founder

Every blend is 100% compatible with Nespresso and Nescafe Dolce Gusto machines, and every batch is roasted and packed in Thailand for maximum freshness.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2019

The Idea

After years of paying over the odds for branded capsules, our founder started experimenting with compatible blends from a home kitchen in Phrakanong.

Cafecaps is Born

We opened our shop on Sukhumvit 40 with just 6 blends. Word spread fast — Bangkok's coffee drinkers wanted something better and cheaper.

Launched nationwide delivery with free shipping across Thailand. Product range grew to cover both Nespresso and Dolce Gusto systems.

2024

Common Questions

FAQ

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes! Free delivery across Thailand on every order, no minimum spend required.

Which capsule systems do you carry?

Nespresso (Original and Vertuo compatible) and Nescafe Dolce Gusto. All capsules are 100% compatible — no adapters needed.

How long does delivery take?

Bangkok: 1–2 business days. Other provinces: 2–4 business days. Orders before 2 PM ship same day.

Can I return or exchange capsules?

Yes, faulty or misdescribed capsules can be replaced or refunded within 30 days. Due to hygiene regulations, opened boxes can only be returned if defective.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! Special pricing for cafes, offices, and bulk orders of 20+ boxes. Contact us at info@cafecaps.com for a custom quote.
