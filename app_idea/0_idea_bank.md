📱 App Idea: Blink

You pass someone cute. Blink — your phone captures the moment and you decide later if it's worth remembering.

[pitch]

A micro-moments journal where your front camera silently logs a timestamped photo every time you press the volume button — like a life-pager you ping to bookmark real life. Revisit your day as a scrolling reel of blink-moments.

Features:
  • Blink capture: volume-down press → front camera snaps in 50ms → silently saved to a private daily reel. Zero UI, zero disruption.
  • Day reel: scroll back through today's blink-moments as a vertical feed with timestamps. Swipe to archive or delete any frame.
  • Memory map: blinks with location data (coarse, neighborhood-level) plotted on a mini map — see where your day happened.
  • Blink streak: earn a "📸 streak" badge for capturing moments every hour during waking hours. Motivates consistent journaling without effort.

💰 Pro: unlimited history + export day reel as a 15s looping video + "Best of Month" auto-generated highlight reel + private cloud sync.
